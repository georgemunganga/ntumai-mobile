# Ntumai Brand Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Document Purpose:** Comprehensive brand identity guidelines for all Ntumai digital products

## 1. Brand Overview

### Mission
Ntumai is an on-demand delivery and tasking platform that connects customers with taskers, vendors, and delivery services. We empower communities by enabling quick, reliable, and affordable services.

### Brand Values
- **Reliability:** Consistent, dependable service
- **Accessibility:** Easy to use for everyone
- **Community:** Connecting people and supporting local businesses
- **Innovation:** Continuously improving the user experience
- **Transparency:** Clear communication and fair pricing

### Brand Personality
- Friendly and approachable
- Professional and trustworthy
- Modern and forward-thinking
- Local and community-focused
- Energetic and dynamic

## 2. Logo & Visual Identity

### Primary Logo

**Logo:** Ntumai (stylized with map pin icon integrated)  
**Colors:** Teal (#08AF97) on white background  
**Format:** PNG, SVG (with transparency)  
**Usage:** Primary branding across all platforms

**Logo Specifications:**
- Minimum size: 40px (mobile), 80px (web)
- Clear space: 10px minimum on all sides
- Do not rotate, skew, or distort
- Do not change colors unless specified
- Do not add shadows or effects

### Logo Variants

**1. Full Logo (Horizontal)**
- Logo with "NTUMAI" text
- Usage: Headers, footers, splash screens
- Minimum width: 120px

**2. Logo Mark (Icon Only)**
- Map pin with Ntumai styling
- Usage: App icons, favicons, small spaces
- Minimum size: 24px

**3. Logo on Teal Background**
- White "NTUMAI" text on teal background
- Usage: Splash screens, promotional materials
- Minimum size: 80px

**4. Logo on White Background**
- Teal "NTUMAI" text on white background
- Usage: Light backgrounds, standard usage
- Minimum size: 80px

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Do not use different colors
- ❌ Do not rotate or skew
- ❌ Do not add shadows or effects
- ❌ Do not change proportions
- ❌ Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- ❌ Do not use outdated versions

## 4. Typography

### Font Family

**Primary Font: Ubuntu**
- **Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** All UI text, headings, body copy
- **Source:** Google Fonts (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Ubuntu)
- **License:** Ubuntu Font License 1.0

### Font Sizes & Styles

**Heading 1 (H1)**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Bold, 32px
- **Line Height:** 40px (1.25x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Page titles, major sections
- **Example:** "Welcome to Ntumai"

**Heading 2 (H2)**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Bold, 24px
- **Line Height:** 32px (1.33x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Section headers, card titles
- **Example:** "Your Orders"

**Heading 3 (H3)**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Medium, 18px
- **Line Height:** 24px (1.33x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Subsection headers, list titles
- **Example:** "Marketplace"

**Heading 4 (H4)**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Medium, 14px
- **Line Height:** 20px (1.43x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Form labels, card subtitles
- **Example:** "Delivery Address"

**Body Large**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Regular, 16px
- **Line Height:** 24px (1.5x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Main content, descriptions
- **Example:** "Your order is being prepared"

**Body Regular**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Regular, 14px
- **Line Height:** 20px (1.43x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Secondary content, helper text
- **Example:** "Estimated delivery time"

**Body Small**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Regular, 12px
- **Line Height:** 16px (1.33x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0px
- **Usage:** Captions, timestamps, metadata
- **Example:** "Delivered 2 hours ago"

**Caption**
- **Font:** Ubuntu Light, 11px
- **Line Height:** 16px (1.45x)
- **Letter Spacing:** 0.5px
- **Usage:** Form hints, footnotes, fine print
- **Example:** "By continuing, you agree to our terms"

### Typography Guidelines

**Readability:**
- Minimum font size: 12px (mobile), 14px (web)
- Line height: 1.5x font size minimum
- Maximum line length: 60-80 characters for body text
- Contrast ratio: 4.5:1 minimum for normal text

**Hierarchy:**
- Use size, weight, and color to establish hierarchy
- Limit to 3-4 font sizes per page
- Use bold for emphasis, not all caps
- Use italics sparingly

**Mobile Considerations:**
- Increase line height on mobile (1.6x)
- Use larger font sizes for better readability
- Ensure touch targets are at least 44px tall
- Maintain consistent spacing

## 6. Imagery & Photography

### Photography Style

**Characteristics:**
- Authentic and relatable
- Diverse representation
- Bright, well-lit scenes
- Natural, not overly staged
- Focus on people and community
- Local context and culture

**Do's:**
- ✓ Use high-quality, professional images
- ✓ Show real people using the service
- ✓ Include local landmarks and culture
- ✓ Use warm, inviting colors
- ✓ Maintain consistent editing style

**Don'ts:**
- ✗ Use stock photos that look generic
- ✗ Use low-quality or blurry images
- ✗ Include overly staged or artificial scenes
- ✗ Use images with conflicting color grading
- ✗ Use images that don't represent diversity

### Image Specifications

**Product Images**
- Format: PNG or WebP (with transparency)
- Resolution: 2x (for retina displays)
- Minimum size: 200x200px
- Background: Transparent or white

**Hero Images**
- Format: JPEG or WebP
- Resolution: 2x (for retina displays)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
- Minimum size: 800x600px

**Icons**
- Format: SVG (preferred) or PNG
- Size: 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px
- Color: Teal (#08AF97) or semantic colors
- Style: Outlined, not filled

## 8. Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Friendly & Approachable**
- Use conversational language
- Avoid jargon and technical terms
- Use "you" and "we" to build connection
- Example: "Let's find you a tasker" (not "Tasker assignment initiated")

**Professional & Trustworthy**
- Be clear and concise
- Use correct grammar and spelling
- Provide accurate information
- Be honest about limitations
- Example: "Your order is on the way" (not "Order might arrive soon")

**Empowering & Supportive**
- Help users feel in control
- Provide clear next steps
- Celebrate user achievements
- Offer help when needed
- Example: "You've earned 500 points! Redeem them now" (not "Points accumulated")

### Tone Examples

**Success Message**
- ✓ "Your order is confirmed! You'll receive updates as it's prepared."
- ✗ "Order status: CONFIRMED"

**Error Message**
- ✓ "We couldn't process your payment. Please check your card details and try again."
- ✗ "Payment failed. Error code: 402"

**Empty State**
- ✓ "No orders yet. Start exploring to find your favorite restaurants!"
- ✗ "No data available"

**Call to Action**
- ✓ "Get your order delivered in 20 minutes"
- ✗ "Proceed to checkout"

## 11. Accessibility in Branding

### Color Contrast

- Text on background: 4.5:1 minimum
- Large text (18pt+): 3:1 minimum
- UI components: 3:1 minimum
- Test with: WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Typography Accessibility

- Minimum font size: 12px
- Line height: 1.5x minimum
- Letter spacing: 0.12em for body text
- Avoid: All caps, italics for long text

### Motion & Animation

- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Animations: 300ms or less
- Avoid: Flashing (more than 3x/second)
- Provide: Static alternative if critical

### Images & Icons

- All images: Descriptive alt text
- Icons: Label or aria-label required
- Decorative: Hidden from screen readers
- SVG: Title and description tags
